Optimizing Technology Spend: Strategies for Success

Rahul Gupta · 2024 · 3 min read

Optimizing technology spend is essential for maximizing ROI and ensuring sustainable growth. These four disciplines come from managing a $1.45B technology budget and saving roughly $400M — while the products kept running and technology-led revenue grew.

Conduct a Technology Audit

Regularly review your technology stack to identify redundant or underutilized assets. This helps in reallocating resources to more critical areas.

Prioritize Investments

Focus on technology investments that align with your business goals and deliver the highest value. Prioritizing investments ensures that resources are allocated efficiently.

Adopt Cloud Solutions

Cloud computing offers scalability and cost savings. By moving to the cloud, you can reduce hardware costs and pay only for the resources you use.

Implement Automation

Automation can significantly reduce operational costs. Invest in automation tools to streamline processes and improve efficiency. Spend optimization is value creation, not cost cutting: fund modernization from its own savings.
